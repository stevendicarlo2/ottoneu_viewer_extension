# Privacy Policy for Ottoneu Game Formatter

## Data Collection
This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

## Permissions
The extension only requests permission to access pages on ottoneu.fangraphs.com to provide visual formatting improvements.

## Local Storage
The extension does not use local storage or cookies to store any user data.

## Third Party Services
The extension does not communicate with any third-party services or external APIs.
